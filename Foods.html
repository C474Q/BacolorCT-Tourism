Sisig

Sisig is a popular Filipino dish that originated in Pampanga. It typically consists of chopped and grilled pig's face and ears, seasoned with chili peppers, onions, and various spices. Sisig is often served on a sizzling plate and is a favorite among locals and tourists alike.
