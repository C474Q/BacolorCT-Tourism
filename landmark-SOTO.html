Juan Crisostomo Soto Statue

The Juan Crisostomo Soto (Crissot) historical marker in Bacolor City, Pampanga, stands as a solemn tribute to the city's historical and cultural roots. Juan Crisostomo Soto, also known by his popular pen name, Crissot, is a Pampango poet who wrote various zarzuelas. He is also attributed to the creation of a Kampampangan Version of Balagtasan, also known as Crissotan
